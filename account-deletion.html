Delete Your Hunger Valley Account

If you wish to permanently delete your Hunger Valley app account and all related data, please contact us.

How to Request Account Deletion

  • Email us at: help.hungervalley@gmail.com with subject "Account Deletion Request - Hunger Valley"
  • Include your registered name and mobile number in your email.

What Happens Next?
Our team will process your request and permanently delete your account within 7 days. All your information will be removed from our servers. You will not be able to recover your account or data once deleted.

For help or queries, contact us at help.hungervalley@gmail.com.
